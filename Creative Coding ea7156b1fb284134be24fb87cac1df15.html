Creative Coding

I/M/D class of 2028, year 1

In the era of move-fast-break-things, modern technologies rewrite our reality, often dismissing critical reflections over who and how gets to do that rewriting.

Creative engagement is crucial to think through the implications of new tech and reclaim its design; a process that requires learning about and tinkering with the technologies ourselves.

In Creative Coding, the artist makes and thinks with algorithms, generative rules, and conditional systems. Code becomes part of the creative process, as a tool for self-expressive and critical ends.
