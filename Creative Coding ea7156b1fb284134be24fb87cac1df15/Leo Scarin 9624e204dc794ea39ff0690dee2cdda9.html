Who am I?

I am an artist and coder based in The Hague, NL. Here, in 2021, I graduated from I/M/D.

Since then, I have worked in the field of creative coding and media art, both with local and international parties.

What am I here for?

I program and lead the Creative Coding program for your class. That includes workshops, assignments, readings, feedback rounds, field mapping, off-site trips, guest lectures, etc.

Inside and outside class you can ask me any advice related to coding, electronics, computers, digital culture, politics of code, and permacomputing.

I normally respond to emails within 48h.

What do I do, where and how?

Artistic Practice

I am an artist working with interactive media. Sometimes moving image, sometimes installations (motion capture, XR, sensors). Some work is autonomous and some is in collaboration with other makers or institutions.

Artistic Research

I also do a lot of research on code, digital culture, and its political and ecological ramifications. You can find me giving talks or workshops about it.

VJ / live shows

You may see me curating and producing visuals for live shows with 3345, Future Intel Radio, Royal Surf Club, or other local events in The Hague.

RGBdog

Together with Soyun Park I run RGBdog, a creative tech studio that researches bonding technology; that is technology to build, maintain, and bridge solidary communities.

We do this through a design practice of publications, playful events, educational contexts, or experimental films.

Educational practice

In 2022 I began teaching in KABK, along with eventual guest lectures in other art academies.

Since September 2023 I lead the TechLab program at the Moving Image department in AKI, Enschede.
